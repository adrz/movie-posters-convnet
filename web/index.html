About

ConvNet-Posters is a project that aims to use the power of ConvNet to cluster similar movie posters.

Scroll to zoom, and click on the thumbnails to select a poster. The 6th most ''similar'' movie posters will appear on the right side.

For sake of readability (and computer safety), only 2000 posters were randomly selected and plotted. To explore all the ~38000 posters you need to use the search engine.
